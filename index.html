about me

I’m a robotics engineer with a passion for creating human-centered technology. I grew up in a small town in Iran, driven by curiosity and a deep love for science—first math and physics, then robotics and innovation.

Over the past 9 years, I’ve worked at the intersection of mechanical design, biomechanics, and robotics. I’m the creator and sole developer of Estella—a humanoid robot inspired by my grandmother’s struggle with Parkinson’s disease—and I’ve also contributed to telerobotic systems like Painverse. I believe in building technology that speaks to human needs. And I follow that belief like a white rabbit—through engineering, storytelling, and innovation.
